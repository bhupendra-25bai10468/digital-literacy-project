# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or emails  
- Always check the URL before entering any personal information  
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone  
- Keep strong and unique passwords for different accounts  
- Activate Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions  
- Keep your apps updated  
- Be cautious while receiving any urgent request for action

## Reporting Cybercrime (India)
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
