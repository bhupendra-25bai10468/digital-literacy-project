# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails  
- Always verify website URLs before entering personal details  
- Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone  
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Avoid using public WiFi for financial transactions  
- Regularly update your apps and software  
- Be cautious of messages asking for urgent action  

## Reporting Cybercrime (India)
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
